CEN 216 Object Oriented Programming Lab

Object Oriented Programming with C++

In this lab, object-oriented programming with C++ will be introduced.

  • Labs will be in Software Lab 2 (Yazılım Lab 2) located in the building across the department building.
  • Lab Hours:
    • For Primary Education:
      Tuesday at 13:15-15:00
    • For Secondary Education:
      Tuesday at 15:15-17:00
  • Lab course begin in 01/03 Monday with an introduction.
  • Lab Points:
    • Report: %40
    • Final Exam: %60
  • You will send your reports to cucen216@gmail.com. The deadline for submission of reports will be announced later.
  • Do not bring any food or drink to the lab.
  • You can use your own laptops during the labs. See Resource section for required software.
  • For any question see Contact section.
